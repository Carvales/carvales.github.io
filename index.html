About my PhD

PhD subject

Neural learning for efficient quadrotor flight control

Completed at Université Grenoble Alpes in GIPSA-lab, in collaboration with CITI-lab.
Under the supervision of Nicolas Marchand, Ahmad Hably and Jilles S. Dibangoye.

Objective : Combine control-command algorithms and deep learning algorithms to improve flight tracking performance of quadrotors.
